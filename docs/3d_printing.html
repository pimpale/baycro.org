3D Printing

We will be accepting 3D printed face shields and masks, however, please adhere to these guidelines:

We prefer that printed parts are made out of the PETG material or other high heat or antibacterial materials. In addition, we prefer if the models printed have been checked by health officials. Examples of these are:

  • Prusa Research
  • UCSF Face Shield
  • DtM Face Shield

Depending on the models you print, the supplementary supplies that are necessary will change. Make sure that if you are providing the extra materials, they are compatible with the parts you print.


Before donating, please go through these steps:

  • Please fill out the form below prior to donating. If you are donating multiple times, fill out the form each time.
  • When donating, please drop off the items in a sealed bag, with the following information:
    • First name, last name, and email address
    • Type of supply created (face shield frames, face masks, etc.)
    • Material used

If you are not using a model that has already been checked by health officials, we will be reaching out you and asking for an STL file of your model.
