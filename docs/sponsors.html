Thank You to Our Current Sponsors!




If you are interested in sponsoring Baycro please contact us through bayareacoronarelief@gmail.com or you can fill out the form below and we will reach out to you.

Any help is greatly appreciated; we currently accept monetary donations, resources, or materials. If you are a sponsor we will feature your party on our website as well as any outreach events. Monetary donations will be used to reimburse our transportation staff, shipping donations, material costs, domain hosting, and website hosting. All of the remaining money will go directly to hospitals and needed organizations. Resources and materials will go directly to our team to send out to front line workers and needed institutions.
