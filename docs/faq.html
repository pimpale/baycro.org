FAQ

Q: Where and how do I donate?

A: Depending on where you live, you can choose where to drop off supplies. A listing of the locations, timings, and contact info for drop off zones and people can be found on our Donation page.

Q: Are there any requirements to donate?

A: Depending on the type of donation, there are certain requirements. If you plan to donate 3D printed equipment, please check our page on 3D printed supplies to see the necessary requirements. If you are donating general supplies, make sure all items are sealed and have never been opened.

Q: What items should I donate?

A: Items that are commonly requested are surgical masks, N95 masks, face shields, alcohol pads, hand sanitizer, and other sanitization products.

Q: I am not from the Bay Area, but I would still like to participate. Are there still opportunities for me to help?

A: Even if you are not from the Bay Area, you can still join. All of our communication is done online and you will still receive support from the rest of the Baycro.

Q: Are there any requirements to join Baycro?

A: Anyone can join Baycro; however, everyone who joins is expected to do the work they are supposed to do. Check out our Join page for more information.

Q: Can we make monetary donations?

A: Yes, we have a Gofundme page.

Q: Where will donations be sent to?

A: The donations that we collect will be sent to anywhere in need. This means they will be sent to hospitals, shelters, and elderly homes across the globe.

Q: How do we get contact for sponsorship opportunities?

A: We offer both an email to contact us or a form for us to reach bad to you. Please look at our sponsorship page for more information.

Q: Is this an affiliated organization?

A: No, Baycro is currently not an affiliated organization.
