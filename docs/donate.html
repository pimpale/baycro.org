Donate

Baycro uses multiple methods to collect donations; the two main types being collection drives and direct donations. In collection drives, we set up a stand for a duration of time and people can drop off supplies from their cars. Direct donations are where the transfer of supplies are coordinated beforehand between the Baycro member and the donor.

In addition, Baycro also accepts monetary donations through our Gofundme page. All money donated will be used towards charity.
