Join Baycro

Baycro is a student led organization, with people in each school conducting their own donation drives to collect as many supplies as possible. Although we are student led, there are no requirements regarding the participants of the organization and who can join. As long as you complete the necessary tasks to collect supplies in your area, you are a valued member in our organization.

If you would like to join Baycro please fill out the form below. If your school or organization already has a branch of Baycro working, we will connect you with them, however, if you are the first person from your school/organization to join, you will have to recruit people from your school/organization to create a small team.
