FAQ

Q: Where and how do I donate?

A: Depending on where you live, you can choose where to drop off supplies. A listing of the locations, timings, and contact info for drop off zones and people can be found on our Donation page.

Q: Are there any requirements to donate?

A: Depending on the type of donation, there are certain requirements. If you plan to donate 3D printed equipment, please check our page on 3D printed supplies to see the requirements necessary. If you are donating general supplies, make sure all items are sealed and have never been opened.
